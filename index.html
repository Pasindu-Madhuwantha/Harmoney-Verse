Literature Survey




Welcome to our research website

In the digital era, the way we consume music has transformed drastically. Harmony Verse stands at the forefront of this revolution, offering an unprecedented abundance of choices and personalized recommendations. Despite this abundance, several challenges persist in the music landscape, hindering seamless enjoyment and exploration of new creative avenues. Our mission is to overcome these challenges by harnessing the power of artificial intelligence (AI). Harmony Verse is a comprehensive web application designed to elevate your music consumption experience. Leveraging cutting-edge AI technologies, we address key challenges such as generating fresh compositions from existing songs, accurately classifying music genres, synthesizing melodies from user-generated vocals, and dynamically adapting music to user emotions. Each feature of our application is meticulously crafted to offer you a personalized and immersive music journey.

Our innovative AI-driven features include the Lo-Fi Generator, which uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze the features of existing songs and generate unique lo-fi compositions, fostering creativity and providing fresh listening experiences. The Music Genre Classification & Trending Visualization module enhances your music exploration by improving genre classification through real-time trend analysis, with interactive visualizations to easily discover trending tracks and artists. The Melody Synthesis from User Vocals feature allows you to create personalized songs by inputting vocal recordings, encouraging artistic expression and unleashing your inner musician. Finally, the Emotional Music Display enhances your listening experience by adapting music to your emotional state through facial expression analysis, delivering music that matches your mood for a deeply immersive and tailored listening experience. Together, these modules revolutionize your music engagement, providing innovative tools for exploration, creativity, and emotional connection.

Research Gap




1
Lo-Fi Music Generator with LSTM model

The research project addresses a significant gap in the realm of lo-fi music generation by introducing an LSTM-based Lo-Fi Music Generator with user customization options. This innovative component responds to the need for efficient and personalized music tools within the lo-fi music domain. By allowing users to generate authentic renditions of their favorite songs, it fills a void in the market for tailored music experiences. Through the incorporation of ambient sounds like weather effects and vinyl crackles, users can personalize their tracks to suit their preferences. The methodology encompasses data collection, feature extraction, and LSTM model training, culminating in a robust solution for creating bespoke lo-fi music experiences.

2
Emotion-Based Music Display with CNN model shapes

AI has transformed lo-fi music production using techniques like Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), but often misses emotional connections with listeners. The research offers a solution: a low-fidelity music web player with emotion recognition powered by a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). This system prioritizes the listener's emotional state for music recommendations, filling a gap by interpreting emotions in real-time. Unlike current systems focusing on past habits, the approach provides a dynamic, personalized experience. In summary, the work integrates CNN models with emotional insights, setting a new standard for personalized music recommendations. It ensures tracks match both the lo-fi genre and the listener's emotional state, enhancing the listening experience.

3
Vocal Identification & Melody generation using LSTM model, images of human brain

Addressing a gap in AI-based melody synthesis, our research focuses on integrating user-provided vocal inputs for melody prediction. Unlike existing methods reliant on manual note entry or instrument-based inputs, our approach accepts spoken audio recordings. This inclusive method expands creative avenues, personalizing the user experience and broadening accessibility to melody creation. By shifting from instrument-based to speech audio inputs, we aim to enhance inclusivity and customization in AI melody generation systems.

4
Music Genre Classification System using CNN model

The existing methods for music genre classification encounter significant hurdles. They often falter in accurately categorizing music due to the diverse traits of different genres, resulting in misclassifications. Moreover, the fast-paced evolution of music trends necessitates real-time analysis capabilities to capture the latest tracks and artists within specific genres effectively. Additionally, the absence of interactive and visually captivating visualization components restricts user engagement and exploration of music trends, hindering the overall user experience and potential insights derived from genre classification systems.

Research Problem & Solution




Proposed Problem

The primary problem addressed by this research project is the limited availability of efficient and personalized music generation and recommendation tools, particularly for lo-fi music enthusiasts. The project aims to develop a comprehensive machine learning-based system that transforms existing music into high-quality, personalized lo-fi versions and recommends music based on real-time emotion detection. It seeks to overcome challenges such as insufficient accuracy in vocal audio filtering, lack of real-time processing capabilities, and the need for accurate music genre classification with trend analysis and engaging visualizations. By integrating user feedback for continuous improvement and ensuring privacy-conscious interactions, the project aspires to provide an emotionally engaging and seamless music listening experience.

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution involves developing a comprehensive web application leveraging AI to enhance music generation, recommendation, and emotional engagement. The application will feature interconnected modules, including a Lo-Fi Generator to analyze and transform existing songs into unique lo-fi tracks, a Personalized Music Recommendation System to offer tailored suggestions based on user feedback and listening patterns, and a Melody Synthesis module to generate complete songs from user-provided vocal inputs. Additionally, an Emotional Music Display module will interpret user emotions using data sources like facial expressions and biometric signals to dynamically display music matching their emotional state. Built with modern web technologies (React Js, Node js, JavaScript , Python) and AI frameworks (TensorFlow or PyTorch), the application will provide an intuitive interface for users to upload songs, provide vocal inputs, receive personalized recommendations, and explore emotionally resonant music, ultimately revolutionizing the music consumption experience by creating a personalized, immersive, and user-centric platform.

Main Objective

Lo-Fi Music Generator with LSTM model

The main objective of this component is to leverage advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze the musical features of existing songs and generate new, original lo-fi tracks. This involves examining various musical elements such as rhythm, melody, harmony, and instrumentation from a wide range of lo-fi music to understand the genre's unique characteristics.

In addition to generating new lo-fi tracks, users will have the ability to personalize their listening experience further. They can select specific preferences, such as incorporating iconic movie quotes or adding ambient weather effects (e.g., rain, thunderstorms, or ocean waves) into the music. This customization ensures each piece of generated music is not only original but also tailored to the user's tastes and mood.

Emotion-Based Music Display

The primary objective of this component is to develop a machine-learning-driven music recommendation system that leverages real-time video capture for emotion detection, thereby providing personalized lo-fi music suggestions that resonate with the user's current emotional state.

Additionally, the system will monitor the user's screen and detect changes in emotional response after listening to the music. This indirect feedback will be combined to refine and enhance the music recommendation algorithm.

Vocal Identification & Melody generation

Develop a robust and efficient vocal audio filtering algorithm to enhance the quality and clarity of the inputted vocal audio, facilitating accurate melody extraction.

Design and implement a diverse and creative melody generation model capable of producing a wide range of musical styles, catering to various user preferences and artistic expressions.

Optimize the melody generation process for real-time performance, minimizing latency and ensuring a seamless user experience during melody creation.

Music Genre Classification System

The main objective of this component is to develop an accurate music genre classification system using machine learning algorithms. This involves collecting and preprocessing a diverse dataset of music tracks, extracting relevant audio features, and training various models such as CNNs and RNNs. The system will integrate with music streaming APIs to access real-time data, classify new and trending tracks, and provide interactive visualizations for users to explore classification results. Additionally, the system will offer personalized genre recommendations based on user preferences and feedback, ensuring a tailored and enhanced listening experience.

Metho dology




This research introduces Harmony Verse's AI-Driven Music Station, consisting of four innovative components: Lo-Fi Music Generation, Emotion-Based Music Display, Real-Time Music Genre Classification, and Melody Synthesis from User Vocals. For the Lo-Fi Music Generator, an LSTM model is trained on a diverse dataset of MIDI files to create personalized lo-fi tracks, allowing users to customize ambient sounds. The Emotion-Based Music Display employs a CNN to analyze facial expressions and recommend music that aligns with the user's emotional state. Real-Time Music Genre Classification uses the GATZAN dataset and a VGG16-based CNN to classify audio tracks into genres. Finally, the Melody Synthesis from User Vocals component uses LSTM networks to generate melodies that harmonize with user-provided vocal inputs, utilizing both .wav audio and kern format melody datasets. This comprehensive approach leverages advanced neural networks and diverse datasets to offer a personalized and emotionally resonant music experience.
